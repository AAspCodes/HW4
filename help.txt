List of all possible commands:

QUIT:
	Quit the game.
	
HELP:
	Display this help information.

LOOK:
	Display the current room's description and contents.
	
TAKE (Item):
	Takes the item and places it in your inventory.

DROP (Item):
	Drops an inventory item into the current room.

INVENTORY:
	Lists all the items in your inventory.
	
NORTH, EAST, SOUTH, WEST, UP, DOWN, IN, OUT:
	Move in the corresponding direction.
